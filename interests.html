Interests

Here at Virginia Tech, I am involved in a couple of activities. I am in a campus ministry, called Chi Alpha. I meet weekly in large and small group settings, and I have made some of my best friends here. Most of my time, however, is spent working for my internship with HokieVision. Through this internship, I help with all the filming and media production for Virginia Tech Athletics. I do filming, video editing, video show production, and many more tasks. I am sure that my new digital writing skills will help with all I do with HokieVision. The time spent working here contributes to the informal communal learning section of the VT shaped student. I am learning through all kinds of hands on experience in the same field that I hope to work in someday.
